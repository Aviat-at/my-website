Golden paths provide **opinionated defaults** so teams can ship consistently. The best ones are
modular, observable, and easy to extend.

## Start with repeatable workflows

Document the two or three most common services in your company. Build templates and pipelines
that set guardrails while staying flexible.

## Treat the platform as a product

Invest in feedback loops, adoption metrics, and clear ownership. Teams will only use a platform
that respects their time.
